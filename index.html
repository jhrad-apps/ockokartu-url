Common questions

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored exclusively on your device. We don't send anything to any servers and have no access to your health records.

How can I restore data after changing phones?

Currently, data is stored only on your device. iCloud backup support is coming in a future update.

I forgot my PIN code. What should I do?

If you have Face ID or Touch ID enabled, use that to access the app. Then you can change your PIN in settings.
